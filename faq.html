Q: What kind of websites do you create?
A: We specialize in one-pager websites for professionals, such as consultants, coaches, and freelancers. These sites are designed to provide a professional online presence and highlight your expertise and services.

Q: Can I customize my website?
A: Yes! While our templates are designed to be visually appealing and functional as is, we understand that each business has unique branding and content needs. We offer customization options such as color schemes, font choices, and content sections to ensure your website accurately represents your business.

Q: Can I use my own domain name?
A: Yes! We offer two hosting options: white label, which means your website will be hosted on our domain, or custom domain, which means you can use your own domain name.

Q: What if I need help updating my website later on?
A: We offer ongoing support and maintenance for our clients, including assistance with updates, changes, and technical issues. We also provide training and resources to help you manage your website yourself if you prefer.

Q: How long does it take to build a website?
A: That's the beauty. Our standard timeframe is 48 hours from the time we receive your completed form and payment. However, this can vary depending on the complexity of your website and our current workload. We will provide you with a more specific timeframe when you submit your order.
